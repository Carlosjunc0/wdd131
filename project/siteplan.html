Color Scheme

The main colors reflect freshness, nature, and cleanliness:

  • Foody Green (#00A86B): used for headings, buttons, and main accents.
  • Light Cream (#F5F5F0): used as the general background color for a natural and soft feel.
  • White (#FFFFFF): used for section backgrounds and content areas.
  • Dark Gray (#333333): used for body text.

Typography

  • Montserrat: used for headings to give a modern and professional look.
  • Poppins: used for body text for better readability and friendliness.
